What is Claim detection?

Claim detection is an AI-powered images and video detection that classify your bottles whether it claimable or unclaim. It analyzes your media content using advanced AI technology and classify claim or unclaim base on a bottle condition in both English and Thai languages.

Key Features

Multiple Image Support

Upload multiple images at once to get classification that your bottles are claimable or unclaim.

Video Analysis

Upload videos up to 50MB to have them analyzed and get an answer

Bilingual Captions

Get captions in both English and Thai languages, making your content accessible to a wider audience without any additional translation work.

How to Use Claim detection

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. 1

    Upload Your Media

    Click on the upload area or drag and drop your files directly. You can upload either:

    • Multiple images (JPG or PNG format, max 10MB each)
    • One video (MP4 format, max 50MB)

    Tip

    You cannot mix images and videos in a single upload. If you want to analyze a video, make sure no images are selected.

  2. 2

    Review Your Selection

    Check the previews of your uploaded files. You can remove any file by hovering over its preview and clicking the X button.

  3. 3

    Generate Captions

    Click the "Generate Captions" button. The system will begin analyzing your media, which may take a few moments depending on the size and number of files.

    Note

    During the analysis, please do not leave the page or refresh your browser.

  4. 4

    View your result

    Once processing is complete, you'll see two caption boxes:

    • English Caption: A detailed description in English
    • Thai Caption: The same description translated into Thai

    You can copy each caption using the copy button in the top-right corner of each caption box.

  5. 5

    Check Usage Statistics

    At the bottom of the results, you'll see AI usage statistics showing:

    • Input Tokens: How much data was processed
    • Output Tokens: How much text was generated
    • Total Cost: The equivalent cost in USD and Thai Baht

Best Practices

For Best Results with Images:

  • Use clear, high-quality images - While Claim detection can handle most images, clearer pictures yield better analysis.
  • Group related images together - When uploading multiple images, make sure that all of the images is the same bottle.
  • Balance quantity - Uploading 3-5 images typically gives the best results.

For Best Results with Videos:

  • Keep videos concise - Shorter videos (around 30 seconds) tend to get more focused captions.
  • Ensure good lighting and clarity - The AI analyzes visual elements, so video quality matters.
  • Consider the content - Videos with clear subjects and actions will receive more precise answer.
